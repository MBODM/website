Hi, my name is Marcel. I´m a software developer. I live and work in the south of Germany.

Since the late 90´s my heart belongs to software development and in my career i was active as developer, architect and consultant. For many years i developed software with .NET and C#. Mostly web solutions with ASP.NET MVC and the Web API, for websites, webapps, microservices, backend and domain logic, or solutions for Windows using WPF (or Windows.Forms, in a long forgotten past).

Since 2016 i also did a lot of JavaScript-related web development, because i like the more functional-driven approach of JS and i like the nature of the modern web. Of course i´m also a friend of TypeScript, since a strong-typed JS can be rather helpful in the backend corners. I also did different native mobile development projects on iOS and Android, over the years.

Besides this main interests i also meet with friends, to sit in the sun and drink some cold beer. If the sun don´t shine i practise one of my favorite hobbies and play strategy board games or join some pub quiz. Since i would definitely call myself a cineaste, you also can find me often in movie theaters. As a child of the internet generation i spend a lot of time to read forums, blogs, Twitter and so on. But sometimes i have to “Esc” from there and get some more relaxing input. Then i read some books, printed on good ol´ real paper.

What i am:

  • A rather enthusiastic but focused person.
  • Affine to "the cool new shit" and also to "solve the problem, no matter what".
  • More of a "make it right"-guy, than a "make it as fast as possible"-guy.
  • More of a "Steve Wozniak"-like type of employee (in short: a nerd 😉).

What i am not:

  • A salesperson (i´m way better in building things, than selling things).
  • A designer (while my CSS knowledge is rather solid, my UI´s are sparse.).
  • Some admin, engineer or DevOps person. I´m a software developer in the first place.

What i´m interessted in:

  • Quality over quantity.
  • JavaScript/TypeScript-based cloud, full-stack and web-backend development.
  • .NET/C# domain-logic and web-backend development.
  • Teams with some relatively high skill level.
  • Working with huge legacy projects, when designed with that in mind (i.e. SOLID, etc).

What i´m not interessted in:

  • Fulltime legacy Desktop development.
  • C, C++, Pascal, Python and Java (for the most parts).
  • Fulltime AI and Machine Learning development or engineering.
  • Huge amounts of administration/engineering or DevOps tasks, in my daily work.
  • Fixing and patching in huge legacy projects, when not designed for longterm maintaining.
